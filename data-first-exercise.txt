A site for pet-sitting (watching someone’s pet while they’re gone). People can babysit for multiple pets and pets can have multiple petsitters.

Pet
-name (string)
-type (string) [inclusion: { in: %w(cat dog turtle)}]
*belongs_to :owner class_name :user
*has_many: sitters through: Agreement foreign_key: pet_id

User
-name (string)
*has_many: pets [optional: true]
*has_many: sitting_pets  through: Agreement foreign_key: sitter_id [optional: true]

Agreement
-start-date (date)
-end-date (date)
*belongs_to: pet
*belongs_to: sitter, class_name: user
